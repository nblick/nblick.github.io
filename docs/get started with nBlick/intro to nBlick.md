# Introduction to nBlick

nBlick helps you understand how your brand appears inside AI models. It shows how often your brand is mentioned, how it is described, and how AI talks about your products. This gives you a clear view of your visibility in the new AI‑driven world.

AI models shape how people search, learn, and make decisions. nBlick makes this world easier to understand by turning AI responses into simple, useful insights.

## What nBlick Does

nBlick studies how large language models (LLMs) talk about your brand. It collects answers from many AI models, checks how they describe you, and turns that information into clear metrics.

These metrics help you:

- See how AI feels about your brand  
- Track how often you appear in AI answers  
- Understand the words and themes linked to your brand  
- Compare your visibility with competitors  
- Follow changes over time  

This gives you a full picture of your brand’s presence in AI systems.

## Why AI Visibility Matters

AI models are becoming the main way people get information. When someone asks an AI about a product, a brand, or a topic, the model chooses what to mention and how to describe it.

This means:

- If AI talks about your brand often, people will see you more  
- If AI uses positive language, trust grows  
- If AI ignores your brand, you lose visibility  
- If AI uses wrong or outdated information, your image can suffer  

nBlick helps you stay in control by showing what AI models say about you today.

## The Core Metrics in nBlick

nBlick focuses on three simple but powerful signals:

1. Brand Sentiment
2. Share of Voice
3. Brand Keywords

You can check the "Metrics Overview" document for a more in detail explanation.

## How nBlick Collects Data

nBlick runs daily checks across all tracked AI models.  
It sends questions, reads the answers, and studies how the model talks about your brand.

The system uses three types of information:

- **AI Responses** : direct answers from LLMs  
- **Cited Sources** : articles and documents mentioned by the AI  
- **Trends Over Time** : changes across days or weeks  

This creates a stable and reliable view of your AI visibility.

## Understanding Your Results

Each metric gives you a different type of insight.

| Metric | What It Shows | What to Look For |
|--------|----------------|------------------|
| **Brand Sentiment** | Tone and attitude | Rising scores and positive language |
| **Share of Voice** | How often you appear | Growth over time vs. competitors |
| **Brand Keywords** | Themes linked to your brand | Strong, relevant, on‑brand terms |

Together, these signals help you see how AI models shape your brand image.

## Comparing Across AI Models

Every AI model is trained differently.  
This means your brand may appear more often in one model than another.

Comparing models helps you:

- Spot where your brand is strongest  
- Find models where visibility is low  
- Understand differences in tone and keywords  
- Plan actions to improve your AI presence  

This gives you a full, multi‑model view of your brand.

## What You Can Do With nBlick

nBlick helps teams:

- Track brand health in AI  
- Monitor competitors  
- Discover new opportunities  
- Fix misinformation  
- Improve product visibility  
- Guide marketing and communication strategies  

It turns AI behavior into clear, simple insights you can act on.

nBlick is built to help you stay visible, trusted, and understood in a world where AI shapes how people discover brands.
