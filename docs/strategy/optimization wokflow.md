# Optimization Workflow

nBlick works in a simple loop.

You:

1. Create an analysis  
2. Track prompts  
3. Generate content  
4. Improve visibility  
5. Repeat  

This cycle helps you increase your AI visibility over time.

# The nBlick Optimization Loop

Analysis → Prompts → Content → Optimization → Repeat

Each step builds on the previous one.

## Step 1: Create an Analysis

An **analysis** defines:

- Your brand  
- Your website  
- Your market  
- Your competitors  
- Your country and language  

This tells nBlick what to track.

Without an analysis, there is nothing to measure.

> 💡 Think of the analysis as your strategy setup.

## Step 2: Track Prompts

Prompts are the questions AI tools answer about your market.

For each prompt, nBlick shows:

- The AI answer  
- The sources cited  
- If your brand is mentioned  
- If competitors are mentioned  

This step helps you find:

- Visibility gaps  
- Weak topics  
- Competitor advantages  

> 💡 Prompts show you where you stand.

## Step 3: Generate Content

Once you see weak areas, take action.

Go to **Content Generation**.

Select:

- The category  
- The prompts you want to improve  
- Language and word range  

nBlick generates an optimized article designed to:

- Increase citation chances  
- Improve AI visibility  
- Strengthen topic authority  

> 💡 Content turns insights into action.

## Step 4: Optimization

After publishing your content:

- Wait for AI models to update
- Re-check your prompts
- Compare visibility over time

You may see:

- New citations  
- Better brand mentions  
- Reduced competitor presence  

If results are still weak:

- Improve content depth  
- Add more authority signals  
- Target additional prompts  

# The Continuous Loop

Optimization is not one-time.

You repeat the cycle:

1. Monitor prompts  
2. Identify gaps  
3. Generate content  
4. Improve authority  
5. Measure again  

Over time, this builds:

- Stronger brand presence  
- Higher AI citations  
- Better competitive positioning  

# Example Workflow

Example:

1. You track 20 prompts about "Apple accessories in France"
2. You see competitors cited, but not your website
3. You generate optimized content for those prompts
4. You publish the article
5. You monitor results
6. Your brand begins appearing in AI citations

That is the optimization loop in action.

# Best Practices

- Start with high-intent prompts  
- Focus on topics where competitors dominate  
- Use recommended word ranges  
- Re-check prompts regularly  
- Optimize continuously  

Small improvements compound over time.

# Summary

The nBlick workflow is simple:

**Analysis → Prompts → Content → Optimization → Repeat**

Measure first.  
Act second.  
Improve continuously.

This is how you grow AI visibility.
