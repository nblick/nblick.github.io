    Welcome to nBlick Documentation¶

    Welcome to the official documentation for nBlick — the AI Brand Visibility platform built for the LLM era.

    As search shifts from Google to AI assistants like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Mistral, DeepSeek, and others, brands face a new question:

    How is my brand being represented inside AI-generated answers?

    nBlick gives you that visibility.

    Why nBlick Exists¶

    Large Language Models are becoming the new discovery layer of the internet.
    Customers now ask AI:

    • "What's the best tool for X?"
    • "Which company should I choose for Y?"
    • "Compare Brand A vs Brand B"
    • "What are alternatives to Z?"

    If your brand isn't mentioned — or is mentioned incorrectly — you're invisible.

    nBlick helps brands and companies:

    • Track how often they are mentioned across every major LLM
    • Understand how they are described
    • Monitor competitor presence
    • Detect positioning gaps
    • Measure AI visibility over time

    We do for AI answers what SEO tools did for search engines.

    What nBlick Tracks¶

    nBlick continuously monitors and analyzes responses across:

    • ChatGPT
    • Gemini
    • Perplexity
    • Claude
    • Mistral
    • DeepSeek
    • And emerging LLM platforms

    Every day, we generate queries, collect responses, and transform them into structured, actionable insights.

    Daily Metrics & AI Visibility Analysis¶

    nBlick runs automated analysis pipelines daily to compute:

    • Brand Mention Rate — How often your brand appears
    • Share of AI Voice — Visibility vs competitors
    • Sentiment & Positioning Signals
    • Category Association Trends
    • Ranking Presence in AI Recommendations

    This allows you to:

    • Track progress over time
    • Measure the impact of marketing or PR campaigns
    • Identify new growth opportunities
    • Detect sudden drops or competitor surges

    Built for the AI Discovery Era¶

    Traditional analytics tools weren't designed for generative AI.
    nBlick is.

    We help marketing, brand, and growth teams answer:

    • Are we visible inside AI?
    • Are we recommended?
    • Are competitors outranking us?
    • Is our positioning consistent?

    AI is now a distribution channel.
    nBlick helps you measure and optimize it.

    • How nBlick structures metrics and analysis
    • How to interpret your dashboard
    • Best practices for improving LLM presence
